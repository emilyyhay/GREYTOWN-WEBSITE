FOOD ACCOMADATION SHOPS & PLACES HISTORY

FOOD & EATERY

MEENEEZ SWEET TREATS AND GELATO

At 92 Main Street, you will come across an amazing cafe, famous for there gelato and hot waffles but also sell, milkshakes, bagels, coffee and even local NZ art!

MORE INFO


MAIN STREET DELI CAFE

At 88 Main Street, you find a great spot to have some brunch or to get a quick something to eat or drink. The Main Street Deli is an iconic cafe with a beautiful outside dinning area out the back.


AROMA INDIA

At 74-76 Main Street, there is Aroma India, an indian restaurant with the best butter chicken and even a delivery service to get their delicous food right to your door step.


THE OFFERING

At 65 Main Street you will come across a little cafe with a gorgeous inside and outside sitting area calld The Offering. They have amazing bakery and other food options as well as there large delicious milkshakes!


THE WHITE SWAN

At 109 Main Street, there is a stunning local restaurant & hotel in one. The White Swan is great place to sit down with family and have a great meal or even hang out with your friends to have a late night drink.


THE 1860 RESTAURANT

At 33 Main Street, you will come across the 1860 Restaurant. The 1860 Restaurant is connected with the Top Pub, so if you wanted a drink you got it and if you want a lovely dinner there is room for that to just on the other side!


CAHOOTS CAFE

At 97 Main Street, this local cafe is a perfect place if you want something on go with their bakery items and hot coffees, but have tables if you want to dine in.


GREYTOWN FISH SUPPLY

You want fish and chips? Well at 114 Main Street you will get exactly that!


PINOCCHIO

At 83 Main Street, you will find Pinocchio, a family owned restaurant that serves amazing in a cozy environment!


GREYTOWN KEBAB

At 95 Main Street, there is a delicous kebab dine in and delivery place!


CUCKOO PIZZA

At 128 Main Street, say hello to pizza! Cuckoo Pizza has a dine in and takeaway service and the even do desserts! So what topping are you getting?


CORNER CAFE

At 154 Main Street, there is another cafe where if you want something on the go you got it! The Corner Cafe have a great selection of food items and drinks!


THE FRENCH BAKER

At 81 Main Street, start off your day with a freshly baked pastry and a hot cup of coffee from The French Baker!
