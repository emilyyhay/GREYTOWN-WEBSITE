MEENEEZ SWEET TREATS AND GELATO

At 92 Main Street, you will come across an amazing cafe, famous for there gelato and hot waffles but also sell, milkshakes, bagels, coffee and even local NZ art!


MAIN STREET DELI CAFE

At 88 Main Street, you find a great spot to have some brunch or to get a quick something to eat or drink. The Main Street Deli is an iconic cafe with a beautiful outside dinning area out the back.
